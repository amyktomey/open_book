Open Book Confections


Through the year, Liz creates unique handmade chocolates and bonbons to treat tastebuds... Last season our own local Fire station recieved the first taste of her delightful creations. These change with the seasons, holidays and special occasions.
Open Book combines a variety of flavors (not all in the same candy, of course) including:

  • berries
  • mint
  • bourbon
  • whiskey
  • champagne
  • jalepeno

There are more, but the list is quite long. If you are looking for a specific combination, send us a Special Request.

Advance notice is required for custom candies. A surcharge may apply. We may require a tasting before completing an order and the recipie will remain the property of “Open Book Confections”.
