Open Book Catering

Dinner Parties & Events


Open Book Catering will give customers the option of at least three different experiences taken from the pages of books and stories. I will use these to create an atmosphere reflecting particular genres. This ‘immersion experience’ will include costumes, décor, music and most importantly, food. We will offer Fantasy, Mythological, and Historical experience. Each dining experience will be served as a 3 to 5 course meal depending on the client’s budget.
