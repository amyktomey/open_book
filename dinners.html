Open Book Catering gives customers the option of three distict genres: Fantasy, Mythology, and History... all inspired by books and stories in my collection. This ‘immersion experience’ will include costumes, décor, music and most importantly, food. Dining experiences will be served as a 3 to 7 course meal depending on package selected.

THAI MENU

  1. Green Papaya Salad: Shredded papaya with fish sauce, lime juice, chiles
  2. Red Curry: Hot soup made with seasoned chili paste and shrimp served with jasmine rice
  3. Tom Yam: Hot and Sour shrimp soup
  4. Yam: A side salad of fresh herbs, protein of your choice(pork/shrimp) and dressing
  5. Beef Guay Teow: Rice noodle soup with chunks of beef, topped with chiles and fish sauce
  6. Tom Kha Gai: Spicy coconut chicken soup
  7. Kai Jeow: Traditional Thai fried omelet
